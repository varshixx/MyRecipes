Instant Mug Cake Recipe

In under 5 minutes, you can have the Best-Ever Chocolate Mug Cake recipe imaginable! Using only a small amount of ingredients you're likely to have on-hand, just mix in a mug and bake in the microwave!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large microwave-safe mug
  2. Stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
  3. Cook in microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
  4. For a healthier version, use 1 1/2 tablespoons of plain unsweetened applesauce and use 3 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon water instead of the milk.
